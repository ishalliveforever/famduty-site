Support

We want FamDuty to feel easy for families to use.

If you need help, want to report an issue, or want to ask about TestFlight access, send us an email and we’ll point you in the right direction.

Contact

Email: famdutyapp@gmail.com

Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue if you’re reporting a bug.

What we can help with

  • Setup questions for parents, co-parents, or child profiles
  • Shared family device mode questions
  • Chore, reward, and sync troubleshooting
  • Feedback and TestFlight interest

Response goal

We aim to respond as quickly as possible and keep support clear, helpful, and family-friendly.

Helpful details

What to include in a support email

Device and iOS version

This helps narrow down device-specific issues and reproduce problems faster.

What screen you were on

For example: entry screen, parent mode, rewards, profile, or shared family device mode.

What you expected to happen

A short explanation of what you were trying to do makes troubleshooting much easier.

Screenshots if available

If you can attach a screenshot, it often speeds up support dramatically.
