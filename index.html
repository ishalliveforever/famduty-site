What is FamDuty?

A family routine app built around real parent and kid flows

FamDuty is designed for families who want chores to feel structured, fair, and a little more motivating. Parents can set up the family, add kids, create chores, review completed work, and manage rewards. Kids can unlock their own profile, complete tasks, watch points grow, and work toward rewards they care about.

The app also supports a shared family device mode, so one iPhone or iPad can work as a family dashboard at home. For households using multiple devices, FamDuty is built around family syncing and role-based access for parents and kids.

Templates that save time

Set up recurring chores once, then reuse them for normal weekly routines.

Rewards that feel earned

Points, cash-out options, reward requests, and parent approval all live in one flow.

Features

Built for both sides of the family routine

For Parents

Stay in control without micromanaging

  • Create the family, invite a co-parent, and add child profiles.
  • Build chore templates for repeatable routines like room resets, trash night, and dog walks.
  • Assign chores with due rules, repeating schedules, and optional photo proof.
  • Approve completed chores, send them back with notes, and adjust points when needed.
  • Manage reward options, approve reward redemptions, and send family announcements.
For Kids

Make daily progress feel visible and rewarding

  • Unlock a personal profile with a passcode and optional Face ID on that device.
  • See today’s chores, track points, and watch streaks build over time.
  • Customize a profile with an avatar, photo, and playful status message.
  • Browse a rewards shop, cash out points, and suggest new reward ideas.
  • Celebrate wins with badges, history, and clear feedback from parents.

Questions Families Ask

Simple answers before launch

Can one device work for the whole family?

Yes. FamDuty includes a shared family device mode so one household device can act as a central family dashboard.

Can parents and kids have separate access?

Yes. Parents enter parent mode, and kids unlock only their own profile and routine view.

Is it built around chores only?

No. FamDuty is also designed around approvals, rewards, streaks, profile customization, and daily family rhythm.

Can App Store links be added later?

Yes. The layout already leaves room for TestFlight and App Store buttons whenever those links are ready.

Shared Family Device Mode

One device at home. Separate access for everyone.

FamDuty includes a shared family device mode for households that want one central phone or tablet in the kitchen, mudroom, or family room. From the entry screen, parents can enter parent mode and kids can choose their own profile.

This makes it easy to keep family routines visible without handing out a separate device to every child. It also keeps parent controls protected while still giving kids a simple, friendly way to check chores and rewards.
